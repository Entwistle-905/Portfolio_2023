About Me

Have you ever seen a magnificent game scene, like ‘God of War Ragnarok’ and ‘Destiny 2‘?
Those scenes make me think I’m in that world and make me want to explore more.
I love to make a wonderful realistic world in a virtual space.
I remember when I first made my own world in Unreal Engine 4. I was so excited to explore my own world.

Hello, I’m Soomin Lee from South Korea.
Before I came to Canada, I studied 3DS MAX, ZBrush, Substance Material and Unreal Engine.
I worked in a company and at the graduate school as a graphic designer for half a year in Korea.
I’m now studying Game Programming at St. Lawrence College and learning C++, HTML, Unity and OpenGL.
Nowadays I’m interested in Modular Modeling and VFX with Houdini and Niagara.
I’m proud of my 3D environment art and design.
I’m always passionate to learn new technologies and tools
and I’d like to use both programming and graphics for 3D modeling.
I want to create a splendid open world game with my assets and design.

I posted my works in ArtStation and my personal portfolio page.
If you are interested in my work, please contact me with 'contact' page.
